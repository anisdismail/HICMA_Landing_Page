HICMA


Welcome To HICMA! The Handwriting Identification for Calligraphy and Manuscripts in Arabic project, abbreviated as the HICMA project, is a new initiative led by the School of Engineering at the Lebanese American University, Byblos, Lebanon. It aims to fulfill the need for a reliable, open-source, and open-access dataset in the field of Arabic Text Recognition using Artificial Intelligence.
